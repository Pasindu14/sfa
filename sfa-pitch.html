Built for distributors

Your own portal to run the whole business

This isn't just a rep app you watch from the outside. As a distributor you get your own secure login with real control over your stock, your orders, your bills and your cash — all in one place.

01

Confirm your deliveries

When stock arrives from the company, you confirm it on screen (your Goods Receipt). The moment you confirm, your stock count goes up automatically — no manual entry, no mismatch.

02

Order more stock easily

Raise a purchase order yourself and follow it through every step — submitted, approved, finalised — with a clear record of who approved what and when.

03

See your stock in real time

Know exactly what you hold at any moment — both normal stock and free-issue (FOC) stock are tracked separately, so nothing gets mixed up.

04

You approve the bills

Every sale your reps make comes to you for review. Approve the genuine ones, reject the wrong ones with a reason. Nothing hits your books without your say-so.

05

Track every rupee of cash

See which bills are cash-collected and which are still pending settlement. No more guessing what's been paid and what's outstanding.

06

Stock always adds up

The system automatically checks your stock ledger against actual counts and flags any difference. Plus a full "bin card" statement shows every movement — opening, receipts, sales, returns, damage, closing.

07

Easy stock-taking

When it's time to count, enter your physical figures right in the portal. The system compares them to its records and sorts out the adjustments for you.

08

Pricing made for you

Your category (A/B/C/D), trade discounts and commissions are built in — so the prices you see are your prices, applied correctly every time.

09

All your outlets, mapped

See every outlet you serve, with location on a map, credit limits and last-billed dates — so you always know your coverage and who needs a visit.

10

Targets & instant alerts

Track sales targets against actuals, and get instant phone notifications for approvals, stock alerts and order updates — so nothing slips through.

🛡️

The bottom line for you: your stock is accurate, your bills can't sneak through without approval, duplicate bills are blocked automatically, and every change is on record. You stay fully in control of your business — and your money.
